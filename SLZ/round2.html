How did you do? Was it hard to hear the melodies? If you don't speak a language with tone, it can be difficult to tell the difference between one melody or another! But in languages with tone like Santiago Laxopa Zapotec, it's just another part of daily language use.

This game was put together by Nido de Lenguas, a collaboration between Senderos and linguists at UC Santa Cruz. The recordings you heard during this game were made by Maestra Fe Silva-Robles of Santiago Laxopa and Santa Cruz, CA, the co-founder of our parent organization Senderos.Our mission is to share the beauty and value of Oaxacan languages like Santiago Laxopa Zapotec.
